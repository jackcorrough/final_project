Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience while using our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you access and use our website.

What Cookies We Use

We use Google Analytics cookies to analyze how our website is being used and to improve our website. These cookies collect information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse our website, these cookies may collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to our website, and information about how you interact with our website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your experience, to understand how our website is being used, and to improve our website. The information collected by cookies is used to analyze usage patterns, to improve the website, and to provide personalized content and advertising. We do not use cookies to collect personal information about you.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our website.

If you wish to prevent the use of Google Analytics cookies, you can install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. The add-on prevents Google Analytics JavaScript (ga.js, analytics.js, and dc.js) from sharing information with Google Analytics about visits activity.
